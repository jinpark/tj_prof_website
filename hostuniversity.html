Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith was established on Feb. 10th 1921. It. is one of the Prestigious State Universities of the Government of Uttar Pradesh approved by the University Grants Commission, Government of India. It is situated just near the Varanasi Cantt. Railway Station. 242 Colleges of seven districts of the surrounding of Varanasi are affiliated with this University.
