FOURTH MEETING ON CONFLICT MANAGEMENT, PEACE ECONOMICS AND PEACE SCIENCE





REGISTRATION AND PRESENTATION OF PAPERS

Scholars/Speakers from different fields interested in the subject of the Conference are required to pay a registration fee of Rs. 700/- (Delegates from India and other developing countries), Rs. 600/- (Students from India and other Developing Countries) and US$ 100 (Developed Countries). The registration fee must reach the Convener of the Conference by November 15, 2012. The registration fee may be paid in cash or bank transfer. Those who are desirous to make presentation must submit their abstracts (synopsis of the paper) before November 20, 2012. No abstract will be accepted without a completed registration form and payment of the registration fee. Participants from abroad will have opportunity to deposit their registration fee on spot.

LANGUAGE

Bilingual (English & Hindi)

HOSPITALITY

• Free board and lodging will be provided by the organizers to the participants who pay the specified registration fee.

• University guest house room (two persons in one room) will make available to the foreign participants on the conferences dates on free of cost. If any participants desirous to stay in Hotel will have to stay on their own cost. Organizer may arrange the same on the request of the participants.

TRAVEL EXPENSES

Because of severe resource constraint, it is not possible for the organizers to reimburse travel expenses. The participants are advised to manage their travel expenses from their respective University, Institution, Organization or other sources.
