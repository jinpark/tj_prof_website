FOURTH MEETING ON CONFLICT MANAGEMENT, PEACE ECONOMICS AND PEACE SCIENCE





THEMES OF THE CONFERENCE

The following topics were covered in the previous meetings. But in the present conference, the primary focus is on the Integration of Peace Economics and Peace Science based on Gandhian philosophy:

  1. Interface of Peace Studies and Peace Science.
  2. Gandhian Peace Studies.
  3. Arms Control.
  4. Nuclear Proliferation.
  5. Peace Science Methodology and Theory.
  6. Democracy and Conflict.
  7. Linkage of Internal and External Conflict.
  8. Ethnic Conflict.
  9. Coalition Politics.
  10. Environmental Conflict and Global Warming.
  11. Globalization and Conflict.
  12. International Trade and Financial Crisis.
  13. Disaster Management.
  14. Terrorism.
  15. Resource Conflict.
  16. Empirical Case Studies of South Asian countries.
  17. Crisis of Identity and Conflict Management.

The earlier meetings were held in India and China. Future meetings are planned in Africa. They were organized in cooperation with many academic and professional organizations worldwide. Some of the papers presented in earlier meetings were published in a book series - Contributions to Conflict Management, Peace Economics, and Development, with Manas Chatterji as the General Editor. If you wish to look at the title of the books included in the series, you may visit http://books.emeraldinsight.com. It is expected that some papers from the Varanasi meeting will also be published.
