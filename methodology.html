METHODOLOGY OF ORGANIZATION OF THE CONFERENCE

The two-day conference will begin with the Inaugural Session on December 18, 2012 at 10.30 a.m. The same day after lunch there will be 15 parallel technical sessions/research committees on the aforementioned themes.

Next day December 19, 2012 from 10.30 am to 1.00 p.m. symposium/special lectures will be organized. Same day after lunch the Valedictory Session will be organized in the afternoon in which the report of the Conference will be presented and recommendations will be finalized.

Those invited will come from different disciplines, eminent scholars and pioneers in the field of the corporate sector and NGOs within the country as also from abroad. They will offer their presentations on one or more of the above given themes. Experts in various areas of the specialized themes will be invited as resources that will not only enlighten the participants with their specialized knowledge and wide experiences in their respective fields, but will also direct and steer the scheduled sessions.
